Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh aims to foster innovation as a key driver of its economic growth by investing in technology, research, and human capital development.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is committed to achieving sustainability by promoting eco-friendly practices, renewable energy, and efficient resource management.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is focused on developing robust infrastructure, good transportation networks to support its rapid urbanization and economic growth.

Empowerment

Bangladesh strives for empowerment by promoting gender equality, enhancing education, and improving access to healthcare and economic opportunities.
