Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

angladesh aims to foster innovation as a key driver of its economic growth by investing in technology, research, and human capital development.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is committed to achieving sustainability by promoting eco-friendly practices, renewable energy, and efficient resource management.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is focused on developing robust infrastructure, good transportation networks to support its rapid urbanization and economic growth.

Empowerment

Bangladesh strives for empowerment by promoting gender equality, enhancing education, and improving access to healthcare and economic opportunities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"Let's challenge each other, listen to each other, and dare to imagine a new world with a new lifestyle consistent with an environmentally safe planet. And build an economy where fruits of technology, economic growth are shared evenly by all people, not monopolised by the privileged few.We need to create a world of three zeros-zero net carbon emission, zero wealth concentration, zero unemployment-by introducing social business to solve people's problems, rather than maximising profit.Children are the ones who shape the prosperous Bangladesh. It is the responsibility of all of us to give importance to the beautiful development of children to build a new Bangladesh."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
